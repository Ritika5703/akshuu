To my incredible soul sister,

Today is all about celebrating YOU — the one who brings endless laughter, comfort, and warmth into my life. Honestly, sometimes it feels like we’re this unstoppable mom and masi duo, talking for hours, sharing duniya bhar ki gossips, and supporting each other like family. I don’t even know where I’d be without those conversations that make everything better.

I still remember those moments when someone said something to me, and without a second thought, you stood right by my side — ready to fight for me, defend me, and lift me up like a true warrior. That kind of loyalty is rare, and I’ll never forget how safe and seen you’ve always made me feel.

From our silly jokes that never get old to those deep, heart-to-heart talks where no topic is off-limits — every memory we’ve created together is a treasure. You’ve been my rock, my partner-in-crime, and my personal cheerleader through it all, and words will never be enough to say how much you mean to me.

On your birthday, I wish you nothing but pure happiness, the kind that fills your heart and lights up your soul. You deserve every bit of love, laughter, and magic this world has to offer. Here’s to more late-night talks, endless gossip sessions, and years of being each other’s mom-masi, soul-sister kind of support.

With all my love,

Your Soul Sister
